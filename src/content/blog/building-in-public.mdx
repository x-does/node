# Building in Public

Every feature, every deploy, every decision — we document it. Not because we're obligated to, but because showing work builds trust faster than any marketing campaign.

## The logic

1. **Trust compounds.** When people see real progress, they believe in real products.
2. **Feedback is free.** Public builds attract opinions before you've spent on user research.
3. **Accountability ships.** When the roadmap is visible, timelines matter more.

## How we do it

- The [blog](/blog) captures decisions and build logs
- The [store](/store) shows what's available right now
- The [apps](/apps) section tracks the status of everything in flight

This isn't a vanity exercise. It's a deliberate growth loop: build, show, learn, repeat.
